Locations and Places to Stay

Our Host Hotel: The Ramada Hotel in Trenton

This year our host hotel is the Ramada Trenton Hotel. The hotel is located right off Glen Miller 401 exit. This site has an indoor pool and breakfast and has offered us rooms for $89.00. The rooms need to be booked by December 30 to ensure reservation.

Some of the more popular places to stay are full already, make sure you check with them early on. Fairfield Inn and Suites will know by the end of December if they have available rooms. There are a few hotels in Trenton that are actually closer to Marc Garneau than the ones in Belleville.
