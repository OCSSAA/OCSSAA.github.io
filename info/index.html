Tournament Info

Rules

Friday

  • Matches will be 2 games starting at 4-4 and go to 25, cap of 27. (Red pool will start at 0-0)
  • Warm-ups will be 5-5 for the first matches of the day for each team. Then the warm-ups will proceed 3-3 for the rest of the day.
  • Matches will start early when possible.
  • Blue and Green will have 5 matches on Friday and a minimum of 2 on Saturday. Red Pool will have 4 matches.
  • Single refs all day Friday.

Saturday

  • Matches 2 - 8 will have single refs, while the rest of the day will be double refs.
  • Matches are best 2 of 3. Third games will to to 15 if necessary.
  • There is no cap.
  • Warm-up will be 5-5 all day as teams may be sitting for 2-3 matches.
  • All teams will have a minimum of 2 matches on Saturday.
  • Games will run ahead of schedule when possible.
